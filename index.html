Dangers to Marine Life

The ocean faces numerous threats from human activities and environmental changes. Some key dangers include:

  • Plastic pollution and chemical waste
  • Overfishing and destructive fishing practices
  • Climate change causing rising sea temperatures and acidification
  • Loss of habitats such as coral reefs and mangroves
Learn More About Dangers

Solutions to Protect Marine Life

There are several ways humans can act to preserve ocean health and marine biodiversity:

  • Reduce plastic use and properly manage waste
  • Support sustainable fishing practices
  • Protect and restore marine habitats
  • Promote clean energy and reduce carbon emissions
